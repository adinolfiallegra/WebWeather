Summary


The pourpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this goal we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API, in order to get information about more than 500 cities.
After working on the dataset we used Matplotlib to sketch the graphs showing the relation between different aspects of the weather vs the Latitude.
Factors tha have been considered are: Temperature, Couldiness, Wind Speed, and Humidity.
This site provide all the information needed to go over our analysis, like explanation, graphs and data.
